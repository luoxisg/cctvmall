## Commercial Rate Card / Cost Assumptions

### Commercial Rules

- Replace Camera uses existing cabling and should not be priced as new cabling by default.
- New Camera Point with Cabling should clearly state Cat5e, PVC, RJ45 termination, and basic testing inclusion.
- AC power point, DB tap-in, LEW works, fire stopping, core drilling, and after-hours access should be checked as exclusions or variation items.
- Elevator camera attendance, shutdown, and lift contractor support should be separated where applicable.
